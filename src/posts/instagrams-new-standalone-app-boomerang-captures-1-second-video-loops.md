[Boomerang looks interesting](http://techcrunch.com/2015/10/22/instagram-boomerang/) in the same way Hyperlapse and Layouts are great apps - a single purpose, focused app that does one thing well but doesn't clutter up the main Instagram app:

> ...today it’s launching Boomerang on iOS and Android. It’s a dead-simple app where you shoot a one-second burst of five photos that are turned into a silent video that plays forwards and then reverses over and over in a loop.

Source: _Instagram’s New Standalone App Boomerang Captures 1-Second Video Loops | TechCrunch_ You can download Boomerang [for free in the App Store](https://geo.itunes.apple.com/us/app/boomerang-from-instagram/id1041596399?mt=8&at=10l4Ki).
