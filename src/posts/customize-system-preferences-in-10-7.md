New to me, and apparently new in 10.7 Lion, is the ability to hid and reorganize System Preference icons. [![Customizing System Preferences in Lion 10.7](https://chrisenns.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/05/Customizing-System-Preferences.jpg "Customizing System Preferences in Lion 10.7")](https://chrisenns.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/05/Customizing-System-Preferences.jpg)

> Just go to View > Customize to enable checkboxes next to each pane that you can toggle.
