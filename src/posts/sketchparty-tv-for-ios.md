### Requirements

It's important to note that you'll need a few things to play SketchParty TV properly.

> SketchParty TV requires one of the following AirPlay Mirroring capable iOS devices: [iPad mini, iPad 2, iPad 3rd and 4th generation](http://www.apple.com/ca/ipad/), [iPhone 4S, iPhone 5](http://www.apple.com/ca/iphone/), or [iPod touch 5th generation](http://www.apple.com/ca/ipod-touch/), as well as an [Apple TV](http://www.apple.com/ca/appletv/) 2nd generation or newer (these are the requirements for AirPlay Mirroring).
